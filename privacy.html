PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2025

Anxiety Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All your expense data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

Anxiety Clock itself does not collect any personal information. All expense data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

2a. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings:
• iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
• Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium users who have purchased "Remove Ads" are not served any advertisements.

2b. RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifier
  • Purchase transaction data (from App Store / Google Play)
  • Device platform and app version

For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

All expense data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device, is not backed up to any cloud service by us, and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Children's Privacy

Anxiety Clock is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@tech-tao.com
